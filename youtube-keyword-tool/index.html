Pro Tips for YouTube SEO Success

Use Your Keywords Naturally

Include your main keyword in: title, first 100 characters of description, first paragraph of description, and naturally throughout your script.

Longer Videos Perform Better

YouTube favors videos that keep viewers watching. Aim for 8-12 minutes for most topics (but don't add fluff just to hit this).

Publish Consistently

Uploading 1-2 videos per week consistently signals to YouTube that you're an active creator worth promoting.

Analyze & Optimize

After publishing, check Analytics to see where viewers drop off, then adjust your future content accordingly.
